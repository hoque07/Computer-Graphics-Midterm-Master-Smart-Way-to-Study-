3) DDA Line Algorithm

Idea, Equations & Pseudocode

Step along the driving axis and increment the other by slope \(m=\Delta y/\Delta x\). Plot rounded coordinates.

  • If \(|m|\le 1\): \(x_{k+1}=x_k+1,\; y_{k+1}=y_k+m\)
